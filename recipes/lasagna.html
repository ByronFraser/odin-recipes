Lasagna

Lasagna which won the 2023 best lasagna award

Ingredients

  • Tined Diced Tomato
  • Lean Beeef Mince
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • Fresh Basil
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Dice onion and add to a pan on high heat
  2. Once Browned add 1 tin diced onion
  3. Simmer for 20min
  4. Add lean beef and basin leaves and let cook for an aditional 20min
  5. Assemble from the bottom
    1. Lasagna Sheets
    2. Filling
    3. Cheese
  6. 2 or 3 complete layers can be made
  7. Bake at 150 for 20min
  8. Add basin apon serving
